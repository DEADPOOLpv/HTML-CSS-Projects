Craft

A craft or trade is a pastime or a profession that requires particular skills and knowledge of skilled work. In a historical sense, particularly the Middle Ages and earlier, the term is usually applied to people occupied in small-scale production of goods, or their maintenance,for example by tinkers.The traditional term craftsman is nowadays often replaced by artisan and rarely by craftsperson.
